## The Strategic Mistake

### What Duolingo Lost

Duolingo's founding motto: "Language learning should be free"

**2015-2023:** Beloved brand built on accessibility, joy, daily habits  
**2024-2025:** Public company pivoting to revenue extraction, perceived as "geldklopperij" (money grab) by users

### The Business Risk

If Duolingo doesn't revert or significantly buff the energy system by Q1 2026, it risks losing 30-50% of its free user base. While premium revenue may sustain the company financially, it will lose its cultural dominance and network effects.

**Stock Market Implications:** If user growth stalls or reverses, investors will react negatively
