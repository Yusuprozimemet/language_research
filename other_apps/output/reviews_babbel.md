### Key Takeaways for Improvement
- **Address Pricing Transparency**: Many users feel misled by "free" marketing perceptions. Clarify upfront that only the first lesson is free.
- **Expand Languages**: High demand for Asian (Korean, Japanese, Chinese) and other languages (Arabic, Greek, Thai). Add Dutch as a base language to appeal to Dutch-speaking users.
- **Fix Technical Glitches**: Prioritize voice recognition accuracy and bug fixes for streaks/answers.
- **Enhance Usability**: Add grammar summaries, customizable reviews, and progress trackers.
- **Positive Strengths**: Leverage praise for practical, structured learning to differentiate from competitors like Duolingo.
