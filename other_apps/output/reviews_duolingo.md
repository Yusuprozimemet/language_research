### Key Takeaways for Improvement
- **Revert or Fix Energy System**: Over half of negative feedback targets this; it kills motivation by punishing progress. Restore hearts or allow energy refill via perfect practice/ads without limiting flawless users.
- **Reduce Ad Aggression**: Shorten/less frequent ads; tone down Super upsells. Offer more free energy for ad-watching to retain casual users.
- **Enhance Depth & Accuracy**: Add optional grammar explanations, vocabulary reviews, and dialect options. Fix speech recognition and AI translation bugs promptly.
- **Improve Usability for All**: Vocabulary lists, test-out skips, family sharing, and privacy controls (e.g., feed opt-out). Expand non-language courses if well-received.
- **Positive Strengths**: Capitalize on gamification, vast language library, and daily habit formation—market as the fun, free entry to language learning, but avoid alienating long-term free users.
